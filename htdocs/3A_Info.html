The Virus Itself

This virus is also known as SARS-Cov-2, which stands for "Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome", a viral respiratory illness caused by a coronavirus.

Yes, there is not only 1 coronavirus. Coronavirus is a family of viruses that cause respiratory problems in humans AND animals. Most cases are mild but with the emergence of SARS (China, 2002 - 2003) and MERS (Middle East Respiratory Syndrome, 2012) they can also cause severe disease.

Coronaviruses have relatively simple structures. They are spherical and coated with spikes of protein to bind with healthy cells and infect them. These spikes, under the vision of a powerful microscope, give the coronavirus their name, as they make a crown (which is "corona" in Latin).

This particular coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2, is believed to come from bats and was transmitted to humans in the end of 2019. It is still not sure whether the transmission was direct, or it was transmitted fmo other animals before landing in humans.
